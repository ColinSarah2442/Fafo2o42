Colin Garber & Associates is a firm

Australia. Our firm deals predominantly in

commercial and conveyancing, covering

various fields of law.

are fixed wherever possible.

Property and business settlements are

charged out at settlement agents' rates.


 We are conveniently located at The Business

Building, 328 Albany Highway, Victoria Park,

close to the Causeway, transport and

ample parking.
